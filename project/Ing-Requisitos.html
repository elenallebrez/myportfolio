Requirements Engineering Project

Documentation and analysis of requirements for a software system

Requirements Engineering Use Cases Specification UML Modeling

View Documentation

The challenge: before coding any system, it is crucial to understand what the users need and what constraints exist. For this academic project, we worked with a real client who required a new website. We analyzed their current processes and designed improved workflows, identifying requirements and opportunities for optimization. The outcome: two detailed requirement documents, including actors, goals, business rules, functional and non-functional requirements, and UML models. These deliverables provide a solid foundation for any future software development.

Technical Details

  • Focus: Requirements engineering, UML modeling, business rules
  • Deliverables: Requirement documents, use cases, conceptual models, constraints
  • My Role: Requirements analysis, stakeholder interviews, documentation
  • Timeline: 3 months, 2025
  • Status: Academic project — documentation only (no product built)

How I built it

  1. Contacted the client and identified the main stakeholders and their goals.
  2. Documented current business processes and proposed improved workflows.
  3. Defined functional, non-functional requirements, and business rules.
  4. Designed UML diagrams (use case, class, activity) to support the specification.
  5. Consolidated everything into a formal requirements document ready for future development.
